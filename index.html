About Me

I am Swapnil Mane, a Robotics Software Engineer with 4 years of experience in agricultural robotics, specializing in developing innovative solutions for sustainability and automation. With a Master's degree in Robotics from the University of Bristol, I have successfully combined technical expertise with real-world applications, contributing to impactful advancements in the field. My skillset spans ROS/ROS2, computer vision, AI, deep learning, and synthetic dataset generation. Additionally, I have a strong foundation in hardware design, including CAD modeling, 3D printing, and rapid prototyping, enabling me to build robust and efficient robotic systems.

Currently, I serve as a Founding Engineer at listt.io, where I play a pivotal role in developing AI and robotics solutions for agriculture. Funded by DEFRA and Innovate UK, my contributions include designing navigation systems for autonomous robots and creating vision-based regenerative land mapping tools. These initiatives aim to enhance grassland productivity, promote carbon sequestration, and align with global sustainability objectives. My role involves bridging technical innovation with practical implementation, ensuring impactful and scalable solutions.

I hold five patents in robotics and automation, showcasing innovations in autonomous spraying mechanisms, precision farming robots, and low-cost robotic arms. My accomplishments are further amplified by multiple scholarships during my Master's studies, and prestigious awards such as IEEE Pune Section Innovator of the Year. I have also won multiple hackathons, demonstrating my ability to deliver impactful solutions under challenging conditions.

My vision as an aspiring PhD researcher is to advance swarm robotics and multi-agent systems to transform sustainable agriculture practices. By leveraging AI and machine learning, I aim to develop scalable robotic ecosystems that address critical challenges in food security, environmental conservation, and sustainability worldwide.
